How Rolaa Bot Works?

AI agents for meetings and streams

Rolaa Bot receives pasted meeting or stream URL, auto-detects the platform, validates access
Rolaa schedules the session at specified time, sets reminders, warms up infrastructure, and performs pre-join checks. It monitors invite changes, handles timezones, and remains on standby until joining becomes possible.
At the scheduled moment, Rolaa auto-joins with configured display name, announces attendance if enabled, and requests consent. It calibrates audio, verifies connectivity, and starts compliant recording based on selected options
